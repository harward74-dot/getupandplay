About Get Up And Play

We exist to make movement fun, accessible, and part of everyday life.

Get Up And Play is built around one simple mission: help kids, families, and communities move more—through games, training resources, and programs that work anywhere.

We’re building a platform where you can discover play systems like Play Vitilla, access training content, and bring activity to schools, camps, and teams.
