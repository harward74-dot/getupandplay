About Get Up And Play

Get Up And Play exists to make movement fun, accessible, and part of everyday life.

We build games, programs, and resources designed to get kids, families, and communities active—without needing fancy equipment or perfect conditions.

Whether it’s backyard play, PE class, camps, or community events, our focus is simple: less screens, more movement, more play.
