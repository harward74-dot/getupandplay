Why Get Up And Play Exists

Movement is getting replaced by screens, and too many kids feel like sports aren’t “for them.” We’re here to change that.

  • Make activity fun and accessible
  • Build confidence through play
  • Support coaches and PE teachers with simple resources
  • Create games and training that work anywhere
