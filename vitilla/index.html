Born From Baseball Culture

Vitilla was created in the Dominican Republic by kids who loved baseball and found a way to play anywhere.

When traditional gear wasn’t available, creativity took over—caps became “balls,” sticks became bats, and open space became the field. Play Vitilla honors that tradition while structuring it for modern training and family play.

More Than a Game. A Skill Builder.

Play Vitilla keeps the complete game in play—so skills transfer to real baseball.

Hitting precision

Barrel control, contact quality, and hand-eye coordination from a small, challenging target.

Reaction fielding

Quick reads, first step, secure-under-pressure fielding that keeps defenders engaged.

Throwing under time

Fast glove-to-hand transfers and accurate throws—made harder by the pace of the game.

Footwork + conditioning

Constant movement and high reps build athleticism without a full practice setup.

Decision-making

Quick choices, communication, and competitive awareness—every rep matters.

Family competition

Simple Rules. Serious Competition.

Learn it in 60 seconds—then the reps (and trash talk) start immediately.

Game Format

One hitter at a time. No base runners—fast rotation keeps everyone engaged and moving.

Play to a set score, time limit, or “innings” depending on your group.

Scoring

Defense must secure the cap before it comes to a stop.

Secure + controlled throw to target = Out.
If not secured before it stops = Run.

What ages is this for?

Great for kids and adults. Use closer distances and supervision for younger players.

Can it be played indoors?

Yes—gyms and covered spaces work well. Make sure there’s a safe zone and clear boundaries.
